Pricing Structure for ConstructZone

1. Seller Fees

A. Listing Fees

  • Basic Listing:₹500 per product (one-time fee)
  • Premium Listing (Featured Products):₹10,000 per product (one-time fee for higher visibility)

B. Commission Fees

  • Transaction Fee: 5% - 8% of the total sale price per transaction (based on product type or subscription level). Premium listings may have a reduced transaction fee to incentivize higher visibility.

C. Advertising Fees

  • Sponsored Listings: Pay extra to get your products featured at the top of search results or homepage for increased visibility.
  • Banner Ads: Purchase banner ads to appear on high-traffic areas like category pages or the homepage.
  • Cost-per-Click (CPC) / Cost-per-Impression (CPM): Pay based on clicks or impressions for targeted advertising, boosting product visibility.

2. Buyer Fees

Buyers do not pay a direct subscription or membership fee to use the platform, but there may be small charges related to specific services.

A. Transaction Fees

  • Service Fee: ₹2 to ₹5 per transaction (small fixed fee to cover payment processing, platform maintenance, and security).

B. Shipping and Handling

  • Shipping Costs: Shipping fees are borne by the buyer, unless explicitly covered by the seller. Sellers can set their own shipping rates or take advantage of discounted shipping partnerships available through ConstructZone.
  • Handling Fee:A small handling fee may apply for large, fragile, or special packaging requests.

C. Payment Processing Fees

  • Payment Processing Fee: 2-3% of the transaction amount (typically applied when paying via credit card, PayPal, etc.). This fee may be absorbed by either the buyer or seller, based on platform policy.

3. Additional Fees and Charges

A. Cancellation and Return Fees

  • Order Cancellation Fee:A nominal fee (₹100) may be charged if the buyer cancels the order after the payment is processed but before shipping.
  • Return Fee:A restocking fee may apply for returned products depending on the seller's return policy (typically ₹200 or 10% of the product value, whichever is higher).

Payment Terms and Conditions

  1. Seller Fees:Subscription fees for seller listings are billed monthly or annually, depending on the plan chosen.
  2. Transaction Fees: Commission and transaction fees are deducted from the total sale amount paid by the buyer at the time of purchase.
  3. Fee Adjustments:ConstructZone reserves the right to adjust fees and commissions periodically. Sellers will be notified of any changes in advance.
  4. Non-payment: Failure to pay applicable fees will result in the suspension or termination of the seller's account and their listings.
